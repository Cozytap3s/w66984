Our Services

Explore our wide range of medical equipment.

  • Surgical Instruments

    Surgical instruments are tools used during surgical procedures. They include scalpels, forceps, retractors, scissors, and clamps.

  • Personal Protective Equipment

    PPE includes items like gloves, masks, gowns, face shields, and goggles. They protect healthcare workers from exposure to infectious diseases or harmful substances.

  • Wound care supplies

    These supplies are used to clean, dress, and manage wounds. Examples include bandages, adhesive tapes, wound dressings, antiseptic solutions, and sutures.

  • Disposable medical products

    These are single-use products that help prevent cross-contamination and maintain hygiene in healthcare settings. Examples include disposable gloves, syringes, needles, and disposable masks.

  • Mobility and Accessibility Aids

    These supplies help patients with mobility or physical limitations. They include wheelchairs, crutches, walkers, and canes.

  • Intravenous (IV) Supplies

    IV supplies are used for administering fluids, medications, or nutrients directly into the bloodstream. These supplies include IV catheters, infusion pumps, IV solutions, and tubing.
